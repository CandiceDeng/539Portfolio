    Problem Statement

    Since students with different specializations always have distinct class schedules, the ones who have evening classes or discussions at times need to go home quite late (after 9:00pm). Some of them, especially young girls, might be caught into a danger situation since the lack of company on their way home. In Ann Arbor, the winter is relatively long, which means the days would keep short for several months during both Fall and Winter semester. Students having classes starting after 5:00pm would be faced with the risk of getting victimized in a dark, cold night while going home by him/herself. Thefts and crimes committed against college students mostly happen at night when the victim is alone. According to “New York Times”, the majority of college students hold the opinion of being “reluctant to venture out at night alone”

    Interaction Design

    • Task1: Reach the registration page
      1. Choose “SIGN IN” by tapping corresponding button
      2. I2 Reach the page marked “CREATE YOUR PROFILE”
    • Task2: Create and save personal profile
      1. Enter name, either real name or preferred username in “USERNAME” box
      2. Enter student ID in “UMID” box
      3. Enter email address in “EMAIL ADDRESS” box, ending with “@umich.edu” is required
      4. Tap the inverted triangle at the tight side of “GENDER” box to get the dropdown list, choose gender
      5. Enter cellphone number in the “TEL” box
      6. Tap the inverted triangle at the right side of “INTEREST” box, choose personal interest
      7. Click “SAVE” button to reach the “UPLOAD YOUR PHOTO” page
